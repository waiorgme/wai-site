# Spec: polish slice (Gate 1)

Date: 2026-07-02. Branch: build/polish (stacked on build/optin-toggles).
Vault sources: `01 Branding` (v3 design system: Bricolage Grotesque display, Hanken Grotesk body,
JetBrains Mono data, IBM Plex Sans Arabic; sky accent; gold = recognition only), audit register
P1-1 (share cards), PERF-1/2 (hero + fonts), A11Y-1 (gold contrast), P2-1 (toggle contrast),
P2-4 (favicon), P2-7 (event image weight), UX-2 partial (portal fonts).

## Acceptance criteria
1. **Branded share card.** A real 1200x630 og:image (dark ink, the REAL logo asset, the v3 type,
   sky accent, traceable stats only: "1,300+ members · 40+ countries"), generated by a committed
   script (scripts/make-share-card.mjs, HTML -> Playwright screenshot) so it can be regenerated.
   Every EN page's og:image + twitter:image points at it (absolute URL); AR pages get an AR
   variant with the same layout. No gold, no em dashes, no invented claims.
2. **Self-hosted fonts.** Google Fonts links removed from Base; @fontsource packages serve
   Bricolage Grotesque (variable), Hanken Grotesk, JetBrains Mono site-wide, and IBM Plex Sans
   Arabic imported by the AR pages. tokens.css font stacks updated to match the registered family
   names. No render-blocking third-party CSS remains.
3. **Portal wears the brand.** The Sora/Inter Google links on /portal and /verify are removed;
   src/portal/ui.ts font stacks switch to the tokens (display/body/mono). (The full Climb restyle
   of the portal remains the design-reset brief's scope; this slice fixes the off-brand fonts.)
4. **Hero LCP.** The home hero image loads eagerly (direct src, fetchpriority high) instead of
   JS-lazy; below-fold images stay lazy. The Unsplash SOURCE stays until Mervat supplies real
   photos (owner item; concept-marked either way).
5. **Contrast fixes.** The membership ladder's gold-on-light text uses a new --gold-deep token
   that passes WCAG AA on the paper surface; the language toggle's active pill text passes AA.
   Gold stays recognition-only.
6. **Favicon set.** A square icon (from the real logo asset, padded, never redrawn): 512 PNG +
   180 apple-touch-icon, wired in Base.
7. **Event images under 300KB** (the two flagged webp files recompressed, visually equivalent).
8. **Tests stay green**; a new E2E asserts the og:image absolute URL on the home page.
9. **View transitions: DEFERRED, recorded.** home-motion.js is a run-once IIFE ported verbatim
   from the adopted v3; retrofitting astro:page-load re-init risks the motion system across all
   15 pages for a cosmetic gain. Do it with the portal design-reset slice instead.
